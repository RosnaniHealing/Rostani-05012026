Psychology & Coaching FAQ

Is this Psychotherapy or Life Coaching?
It is a hybrid. We use Psychotherapy to heal the past/trauma and Coaching to build the future. I move fluidly between these two modes based on your needs in the "Here & Now."
Who is this service best for?
It is designed for high-functioning individuals dealing with anxiety, burnout, or life transitions. It is NOT suitable for acute psychiatric crises requiring hospitalization.
I just want to vent. Is that okay?
While being heard is important, my sessions are change-oriented. We don't just loop in the problem; we look for the pattern and the solution. If you are looking for passive listening only, this might not be the most efficient investment for you.
How many sessions will I need?
There is no fixed rule, but deep pattern-shifting takes time. I recommend starting with a 4-Session Package (Monthly) to establish a rhythm and see tangible results. Single sessions are good for specific, acute clarity.
Do I get homework?
Yes, we call them "Integration Practices." Insight without action is incomplete. You may receive journaling prompts or behavioral tasks between sessions.
Do you prescribe medication?
No. I am a Psychologist, not a Psychiatrist. I do not prescribe drugs, but I can work alongside your doctor if you are currently on medication.
What is your Cancellation Policy?
We require 24 hours' notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Sessions cancelled with less notice are charged in full to respect the reserved time slot.
Is it confidential?
100%. I adhere to the ICF Code of Ethics. Your privacy is paramount.
