Mind & Will Services FAQ

Everything you need to know about our integrative psychology and coaching sessions.

What to Expect

What happens in a session?
Sessions are fluid and integrative. We start with understanding your current patterns (Psychology), then move to actionable steps for change (Coaching). It's not just talking—it's decoding your "Living Patterns" and designing your future self.
How is this different from regular therapy?
Traditional therapy often focuses on the past. Our approach integrates Personality Psychology to understand your unique blueprint, then uses coaching to turn insights into real-world action. We don't dwell on trauma; we upgrade your operating system.
Do I need to be "sick" to book?
No. Our sessions are for seekers—people who want to optimize their potential, not just fix problems. Whether you're navigating career changes, relationships, or personal growth, we help you become the conscious observer of your life.

The Process

How many sessions do I need?
It depends on your goals. For quick clarity on a specific issue, 1-3 sessions work. For deeper transformation (like shifting core patterns), we recommend the monthly package of 4 sessions to build momentum.
What if I don't know what I want?
That's okay! Many clients come in feeling lost. We use personality assessments and archetypal analysis to uncover your true calling. The process itself reveals your path.
Is it online or in-person?
All sessions are online via Google Meet for convenience. This allows us to work with clients globally while maintaining the intimacy of video interaction.

Safety & Ethics

Is this confidential?
Absolutely. All sessions are held in strict confidence. As a licensed practitioner, I adhere to professional ethics and privacy standards.
What if I'm in crisis?
If you're experiencing a mental health emergency, please contact emergency services immediately. Our sessions are complementary and not a substitute for crisis intervention.
Can I cancel or reschedule?
Yes, with 24 hours notice. We understand life happens, and we'll work with you to find a suitable time.
